Myofascial Release helps relieve pain and restore motion

Myofascial Release is a safe, gentle, effective hands-on therapy designed to release fascial restrictions and free the body from pain and dysfunction. Through gentle sustained pressure, an oscillating motion called "Rebounding" and "Unwinding" which is the body's own spontaneous movement, you will begin to release unconscious holding patterns that restrict motion and cause pain.

Myofascial Release for:

  • Back pain
  • Neck pain
  • Headaches
  • Migraines
  • Carpal tunnel
  • Scoliosis
  • Sciatica
  • Fibromyalgia
  • Sports injuries
  • Whiplash
  • Jaw pain (TMJ)
  • Restriction of motion
  • Menstrual problems
  • Neurological dysfunction
  • Infants & children
  • Elderly
